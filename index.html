About Me

Hey, I'm Lee!

Something broken? Let's fix it. I fix tractors, riding mowers, 3D printers, and whatever else breaks. Sometimes it works, sometimes it doesn't - but we always learn something along the way.

You might also see my son Luke in some videos - he helps out and does his own projects too. It's a family affair!
